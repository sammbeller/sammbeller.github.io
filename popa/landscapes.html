Bark Kora

Landscapes

In the Ashtray

A tiny sun With yellow tobacco hair Is burning out in the ashtray
The blood of cheap lipstick suckles The dead stumps of stubs
Beheaded sticks yearn
For sulphur crowns

Blue roans of ash whinny
Arrested in their prancing

A huge hand
With a burning eye in its palm
Lurks on the horizon
